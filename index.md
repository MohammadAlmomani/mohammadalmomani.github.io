# Mohammad M. Al-Momani

**Data-driven monitoring, stability assessment, and control of renewable- and DER-dominated power systems.**

My research focuses on measurement-based stability indices, optimization algorithms, and AI-assisted control methods for modern power systems with high renewable energy and DER penetration.

## Research Directions

1. **Short-Term Voltage Stability and Voltage Trajectory Analytics**  
   Measurement-based indices for early detection and quantification of short-term voltage instability, delayed voltage recovery, and oscillatory voltage behavior.

2. **DER and Smart Inverter Reactive Power Dispatch**  
   Sensitivity-aware reactive power dispatch, smart inverter control modes, and TSO–DSO coordination for voltage support and curtailment reduction.

3. **Low-Frequency Oscillation Detection and Damping**  
   Ringdown analysis, ANN-based damping control, PMU-based modal identification, and oscillation monitoring in renewable-rich systems.

4. **Low-Inertia and Converter-Dominated Power Systems**  
   Virtual inertia, synchronverter dynamics, inverter control, renewable integration, and inertia estimation.

5. **PMU Placement, WAMS, and Transient Stability Prediction**  
   Optimal PMU placement, connectivity matrix algorithms, wide-area measurement systems, and ANN-based transient stability prediction.

6. **Renewable Energy Modeling, Islanding Detection, and Protection**  
   AI-based PV array modeling, islanding detection, and relay protection for compensated transmission lines.
